The Way of Happiness – Reset Rituals

Day 1: What emotion is most present in me right now—and why?

(This brings awareness to your emotional baseline without judgment.)

Practice: 3-minute silence + breathing
“Stillness isn’t emptiness—it’s awareness unshaken.”

Day 2: What am I holding onto that I don’t need anymore?

(A subtle prompt to identify hidden stress or outdated beliefs.)

Practice: Ask 3 deep self-inquiry questions
“What’s one truth I’ve been hiding from myself?”

Day 3: when was the last time I felt genuinely at peace?

(Helps recall a moment of clarity and compares it to the present state.)
“Happiness isn’t gained. It’s revealed—once you let go.”

Day 4: What thoughts are repeating in my mind lately? Are they helping or hurting?

(Invites a gentle audit of mental loops and narratives.)

Practice: “Notice 3 beautiful things today.”
Capture one in a photo or journal.

Day 5: If my mind had a weather forecast today, what would it be? Sunny, cloudy, stormy...?

(Creative metaphor to express how they’re really feeling—makes it playful but revealing.)

Practice: Create your personal 5-minute morning or evening flow
